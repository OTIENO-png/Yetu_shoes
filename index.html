DESIGNER SNEAKERS

As you’d expect, the designs out there are plentiful, but there’s one brand that continues to pump out some of the finest footwear on the market. Who am I talking about? Veja. The Parisian brand has become a major player in the sneaker game, producing a range of sustainable styles that have been worn by the likes of Meghan Markle and Eddie Redmayne. They’ll be your go-to for casual days in the office, as well as weekends in the city.
